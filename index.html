Features We Have

Attendance Tracker

Easily track attendance for individual subjects and monitor your progress, ensuring you stay above your desired attendance percentage.

Analytics Dashboard

Get detailed insights into your attendance trends, including current percentages, future projections, and actionable suggestions to meet your attendance goals.

To-Do List

Organize your academic tasks with our built-in to-do list. Set reminders for assignments, classes, or attendance goals to stay on top of everything.

Toss Coin Feature

Confused about attending college? Let fate decide! Use our interactive toss coin feature for those indecisive moments.

Bunk Planner

Our smart bunk planner suggests which classes you can afford to skip while staying within your minimum attendance threshold, so you can plan your day effectively.

Easy Subject Customization

Add or remove subjects as per your academic needs. Update attendance records with ease, ensuring your tracker stays relevant.
